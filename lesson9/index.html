State police respond to crashes, urge motorists to use caution because of winter storm hitting East Idaho

Idaho State Police are urging motorists to use caution because of the winter storm that's currently hitting East Idaho. State police reported that conditions are particularly hazardous on Highway 20 in the Island Park area where several snow-related crashes occurred Sunday morning. Further details on those wrecks have not yet been released. The National Weather Service says conditions in East Idaho for the next few days will include rain, snow and below-freezing temperatures because of the storm.
